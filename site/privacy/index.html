Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

PE Collective ("we," "us," "our") operates the website pecollective.com. This page describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site or subscribe to our newsletter.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you subscribe to our newsletter or apply to join our Slack community, we collect your email address. We do not ask for payment information or require account creation to browse the site.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit pecollective.com, we may collect standard web analytics data through Google Analytics 4 (GA4), including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (city/region level, not precise)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is aggregated and used to understand how visitors use the site. We do not use this data to identify individual visitors.

How We Use Your Information

Email Addresses

We use your email address only to send our newsletter and community updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties. We do not send promotional emails from partners or advertisers. Every email includes an unsubscribe link that works immediately.

Analytics Data

We use analytics data to understand which content is most useful, identify technical issues, and improve the site. We do not use analytics data for advertising, retargeting, or any form of individual tracking.

Email Service

Newsletter emails are sent through Resend, a transactional email service. Resend processes your email address solely to deliver our messages. Their privacy policy is available at resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Data Storage and Security

Email addresses are stored in a Cloudflare D1 database. Analytics data is processed by Google Analytics 4. We do not maintain additional databases of personal information. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages with Cloudflare CDN. All connections use HTTPS encryption.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4: Web analytics. Google's privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare: CDN, DNS, and subscriber database hosting. Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting. GitHub's privacy statement.
  • Resend: Email delivery. Resend's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts: Web fonts. Google Fonts privacy FAQ.

Cookies

We use cookies only through Google Analytics 4 for aggregate site analytics. We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking across sites, or personalizing content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the site.

Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter or community. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics 4 default settings (14 months). When you unsubscribe, your email is removed from our active mailing list.

Your Rights

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the link in any email. To request deletion of your email address from our database, email hello@pecollective.com and we will process your request within 48 hours.

If you are located in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Contact us at hello@pecollective.com to exercise these rights.

Children's Privacy

PE Collective is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We will not materially reduce your privacy protections without notice.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email hello@pecollective.com.
